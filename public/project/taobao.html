Taobao. Ruralism. Urbanization
By 2014, 21% of Taobao online store owners are living in rural areas and there are over 200 Taobao villages around the country. Taking this thread, the project abandons the traditional way of visualizing Taobao's explosive growth within the industry, and chooses to situate it in a broader context: How can we understand Taobao's success along with the transformation of China? The project designs a game-setting comprised of two sets of pokers, which represent the power of Top-down and Bottom-up respectively.
Taobao. Seller. Buyer. The Party.
Four players - Government, Alibaba, Taobao store owners, Consumers - are imagined as the participants of this game. All data, stories, policies, case studies and other intriguing things are merged into the game. The project uses this game-setting to re-envision Taobao's social impact: playing pokers always means wins and loses, but can Taobao.com become a game without losers?
