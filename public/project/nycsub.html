New York City Sub Rider-Readers
Read in move, data-micro.
The project is comprised of two parts to represent data-micro and data-macro respectively. Data-micro is a public survey tracking subway readers - who constitute a fairly amount of daily commuters - and generating personal travelling maps and stories of their reading behaviors.
Ridership mapped, data-macro.
The second part is a map-based visualization of weekday, weekend and annual riderships of every New York subway stations, which represents the movement of subway riders as a whole across New York city.
