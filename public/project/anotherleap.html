Another Leap

Initiated by Power Station of Art in Shanghai, Another Leap is an exhibition showcasing interactive artworks in both physical and digital forms. As a collective response to the digital shift of our society, the exhibition invites artists, makers, engineers, designers, data scientists and entrepreneurs to deliver projects inspired by emerging social and technological phenomena such as big data, maker culture, freelancing, e-commerce, we-media, robotics, labor migration, etc. Another Leap presents fifteen art pieces that range from social media action art to interactive robotic installations, and it's on show from Oct. 28th of 2014 to Mar. 8th of 2015 at Power Station of Art in Shanghai. Another Leap is co-curated by Fei Wang and Hong Pu, and the participants include: Xiaoji Chen, Rob MacKinnon, Kan Jia, Zhen Liu, Binyuan Li, Link, Shituzi, Another Inc., Liao Li, Hao Ruan, LYCS Architecture, Wei Wang, Harry Wei, Han Dong, Willem Boning, Xing Yan, AliResearch, Ying Yu.

Visit the show at: Power Station of Art, Huayuangang Road 200, Shanghai 200011.

Manifesto

This leap is Another Leap.

Compared to "Great Leap Forward" implemented by the Communist Party of China in 1958, and "one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind" by Neil Armstrong in 1969, Another Leap is a tiny, massive, and bottom-up one. It is a series of collective actions based on individual will. It is a direct practice of "two heads are better than one", an empirical iteration of "1+1>2". It is a collective leap pushed forward by many minds and many hands.
