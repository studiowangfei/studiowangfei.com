Another Taobao Village 2
Buying from villages, selling to cities.
Transaction mapped.
The map visualized all Taobao shipments from top five Taobao villages during 2013.11.11 E-commerce Fest. Seller villages are marked as blue circles while buyer cities as grey, the size of transaction (the number of parcels) is measured by the area of each circle. Different colors represents different villages, and the linewidth varies based on the size of that single transaction.
