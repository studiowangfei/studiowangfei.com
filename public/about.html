Fei Wang

​Fei Wang is a thinker, designer and runner based in San Francisco.
He currently serves as the curator of Another Leap exhibition at Power Station of Art in Shanghai, as well as a researcher of Taobao villages with AliResearch of Alibaba Group.​ He is also the founder of Inside x Insight, a data-driven media embeded in WeChat platform. ​
Fei sees design as a way of communication and he enjoys re-imagining the role of designer in​ an age when "everybody and everything have got wired". Since 2010, ​he has initiated and developed a series of projects that merge data, art, history and fun. Working with multiple institutions, Fei engages the public audience through​​ interactive design, data visualization, urban mapping and graphic story-telling. His research and design have been exhibited and published widely by AliResearch,​ Power Station of Art, The New Museum, Swissnex, VELUX, Shenzhen/Honkong Bi-City Biennale etc​.
Fei received his Master of Architecture from Princeton University and ​graduated with History and Theory Prize (2014). Before coming to the US, he studied at Tsinghua University in Beijing and received his Bachelor and Master of Architecture with Distinction (2012). He was the recipient of 1st Prize of Autodesk Design Competition (2008), VELUX Design Honor (2010), 1st Prize of Chengdu Biennale Design Competition (2010), MAD Travel Fellowship (2011), and the inaugural fellow of PSA Emerging Curator Program (2014).
In his spare time, Fei runs and photographs. So far he has finished four marathons in Beijing (2007/2009), New Jersey (2012), and New York (2013).
